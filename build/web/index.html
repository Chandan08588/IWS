Introduction

Ishani Welfare Society is the name of an organization which is specially made for the purpose of providing education, training and techniques to those person who are hepless and not able to pay the fee for their education. So we are trying to provide education to all those students girls and women. We Go the villages with our team and distribute blankets sweaters and etc to orphans poor and other needy people. We also make camps to others villages and expend our days with them and provide them some training for the technologies as Digital transaction, Cloud Classes,Importance of internet, Importance of education etc.
So we request you to please join us and provide some help as possible to you and share about us to all your friend. And also share this to those needy people so we get a chance to help them and you also get the blessings.
