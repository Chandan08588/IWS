About Us:

   Ishanvi Welfare Society is the name of a Non Profit Foundation. The aim of the Ishanwi Welfare Society is not to earn money, our aim is to give the education to all those students who are not capable to pay the amount of fee for their education. So we started one step for them.
For this purpose we opened this foundation on September 2017. We provide computer education, personolity development, English speaking classes. And if we talk about girls we also provide sewing courses , crafting painting etc for them.
     Some time we went to some villages and arranged our camp there and we also spend our days with them. And those days we explain them about girl's education, computer importance, internet, digital transaction and many more other things too. In winter we distribute blankets and other woolen clothes to them.
    This is our aim.
   So We request you to join with our our foundation Ishanvi Welfare Society, and help us in exploring this.
  Your one time help can change someone's tomorrow
